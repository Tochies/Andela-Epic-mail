HOW DO I SEND A MESSAGE?

On your dashboard, click on the send message menu, enter user details and select send to send instantly or select draft to send later.

HOW DO I CANCEL SENT MESSAGES

On your dashboard, go to your sent messages list by clicking on sent messages. Click on the message you want to retract. Options delete and retract will be shown to you. Select Retract and the message will be deleted from the recipients inbox.

CAN I RESEND RETRACTED MESSAGES

At the moment, you can not. But your retracted messages will be in your retracted messages list for 48 hours. After whhich the system deletes it.
